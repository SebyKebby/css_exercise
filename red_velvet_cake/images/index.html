Red Velvet Cupcakes

This red velvet cupcake recipe yields soft, fluffy cupcakes with a rich chocolate flavor and a vibrant red color. Topped with a creamy and tangy cream cheese frosting, these cupcakes are perfect for any occasions and are sure to impress your guest
